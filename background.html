Background Information

The Flow Map project aims to provide a comprehensive visualization of the movement and distribution of Palestinian refugees over time.

Objectives

  • To visualize the migration patterns of Palestinian refugees from 1948 to the present day.
  • To create an educational tool for researchers, students, and the general public.
